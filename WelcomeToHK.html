About Hong Kong

Where is Hong Kong? Hong Kong is located on the southern coast of China and includes Hong Kong Island, Lantau Island, the Kowloon Peninsula, and the New Territories.

Have you ever been to Hong Kong? Hong Kong is an amazing city, and beyond shopping and dining, there is so much to explore. Bustling markets, historic temples, tranquil walking trails, and the most breathtaking views of Victoria Harbour. Travellers can have an unforgettable trip in Hong Kong. Come on, let me show you how to enjoy traveling in Hong Kong!

Explore Hong Kong

Nature and hiking🗻:

Hong Kong is famous for its beautiful natural scenery. Dragon's Back, Thousand Island Lake, and Tai Mo Shan are some of my favorite hiking trails. Not only are they beautiful and relaxing, but they are also conveniently located within 30 minutes by public transport.

Theme parks🎢:

If you are looking for something fun and exciting to do with your children, theme parks such as Disneyland and Ocean Park would be a good choice, each offering a unique mix of entertainment and attractions.

Local street food🍡:

Don't just go to fine dining restaurants. You have to try the most typical street food like egg waffles, fish balls, and bubble tea!

The night view🌉:

To enjoy Hong Kong's iconic night view, Victoria Harbour and the Peak are good places to go. A stroll along the harbour with its most famous landmark and a dinner on the Peak with the city at night will make your trip unforgettable.
